About Me

Hi, my name is Marcus Gonzalez and I am double majoring in Interdisciplinary Math & Economics and Environmental Studies at Fordham University in New York City. Through my various internships and research opportunities, I have gained a profound understanding in analyzing and drafting corporate sustainability policies and strategies. These experiences have highlighted the importance of balancing environmental protections with profitability, in order to deliver long-term value to shareholders and the broader community. In college, I have focused on clubs and competitions, such as my role in Model United Nations and the Ram’s Economic Digest that sought to provide insightful impact and commentary on contemporary, global issues. These activities taught me how to synthesize complex issues into actionable insights to mitigate risk and clearly articulate appropriate strategy. I am also incredibly interested in research and serve as an editor for the Fordham Undergraduate Research Journal and the Fordham Undergraduate Law Journal. I aspire to take an active role in sustainability and business by working to enhance the understanding of foreign issues and policy on globalizing businesses with a specific focus on sustainable economic development. I am always looking to connect with peers and employers interested in collaborating on research projects or business ventures.
